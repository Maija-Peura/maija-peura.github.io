White Pine National Park

An Experience Awaits

On the northeast tip of Minnesota overlooking Lake Superior, White Pine National Park is an adventure waiting to happen. Although parts of Minnesota are becoming increasingly urban, there is still a quiet, pristine portion of the state waiting to be discovered.

Nestled near one of Minnesota's five largest cities, Duluth, White Pine National Park offers the quintissential northern Minnesota experience. You will find yourself charmed by the unique natural settings and the friendly hospitality that is far beyond "Minnesota Nice."
