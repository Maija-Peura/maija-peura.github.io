Camping at White Pine National Park

Do you love camping in Minnesota? Enjoy getting away from the hustle and bustle of life? Do you find the sounds and smells of the wilderness relaxing?

Camping in White Pine National Park is an excellent way to appreciate Minnesota's rivers and lakes and relax. The best thing about camping in White Pine is you can really rough it, or you can experience all of the luxuries of what has fondly become known as "glamping." It all depends on the type of experience and amenities you prefer. White Pine National Park has private campgrounds as well as state- and federal-run campgrounds.

Contact us at 218-555-1212 or online to
begin planning your camping vacation today!

Sometimes wilderness camping is categorized into “remote” and “park” camping. Whether you decide to pursue backwoods camping in Northern MN or traditional camping at a park, there are various camping essentials you should have on hand. White Pine National Park outfitters, stores and parks can offer plenty of tips and recommend the appropriate equipment and safety gear.

Choose Your Style of Camping

Remote camping. Enjoy nature in the backwoods along White Pine's various lakes and rivers via canoe, boat, kayak or backpacking. Click here to find White Pine National Park Outfitter for all your canoeing and camping needs.

Park camping. The White Pine National Park area offers various drive-up campsites at state/federal camp sites. You can use tents, RVs, campers, etc. The parks vary in amenities such as bathrooms, electric and sewer hookups and more! Click here to find White Pine National Park campground.

White Pine National Park has beautiful wildlife to see, fishing, hiking, birding and you can incorporate wilderness canoeing into your camping trip. There is something for every age and every member of the family. The memories you create will stay with you for a lifetime as your wilderness camping trip allows you to stop and take time to appreciate the outdoors and nature’s bounty.
